Why realism & security matter for agents

Independent research scanning 101 agents found only 7% fully pass trust scoring, with major gaps in delegation and data handling (thepitstop.ai).

Security researchers have shown how structural flaws in open‑source agents can enable supply‑chain attacks (securityweek.com).
